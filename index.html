FAQ

How long does a typical project take?

Most projects take 1-4 weeks depending on complexity. Simple automations can be done in days. Larger systems with multiple integrations take longer. I'll give you an exact timeline on our call.

What does it cost?

It depends on the scope. I price by project, not by hour, so you know exactly what you're paying before we start. Most projects range from $2-15k. We'll talk budget on the call.

Do I need to be technical?

No. If you can explain the problem, I can build the solution. I'll handle all the technical work and make sure your team can actually use what I build.

What platforms do you work with?

Shopify, Amazon FBA, TikTok Shop, StockX, inventory systems, ERPs, Google Sheets—whatever you're running. If it has an API or data export, I can work with it.

What if I just need something small?

Small is fine. Sometimes a single automation or integration solves 80% of the problem. Let's talk about what would actually move the needle for you.
